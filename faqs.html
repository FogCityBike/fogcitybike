Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to make a reservation in advance?

We recommend booking in advance to secure your spot, especially during peak seasons.

What should I bring on the tour?

Comfortable clothing, a water bottle, sunscreen, and a sense of adventure!

Are helmets provided?

Yes, helmets are included with all rentals and tours.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 hours in advance will receive a full refund. Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance are non-refundable.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for groups of 10 or more. Please contact us for details.
